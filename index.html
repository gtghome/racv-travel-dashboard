Key Insights Apr 2026
🏆
Victoria record spend: $46.7B in 2025, cementing 2nd place nationally. Melbourne remains the #1 interstate overnight leisure destination with 3.5M visitors spending $5.9B.
✈️
Outbound boom: Australians took 12.4M overseas trips in 2025, forecast to surpass 13M in 2026. Average spend is AUD $9,800/trip — 15% above pre-pandemic.
🇯🇵
Japan is the standout: +24.9% YoY growth for Australian outbound travel to Japan. Strong AUD vs Yen driving interest. RACV tour partners actively promoting Japanese itineraries.
⚠️
US travel declining: September showed a -12.7% drop in Australians travelling to the USA. High airfares and cost pressures are redirecting demand to Asia. Watch RACV USA product mix.
🌏
China rebound strong: Chinese visitors to Victoria spent $3.1B, +15.4% YoY. India volume #1 nationally for VIC with $808M spend. Key inbound segments for Melbourne.
🛳️
Cruise & luxury touring up: APT, Viking, Scenic cruises all posting record bookings. RACV member segment aligns strongly with 55+ demographic driving premium travel demand.
